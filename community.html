PawSafe Community.

Have a question about zoonotic diseases, pet safety, or how to use PawSafe in your classroom or clinic? Ask it here. Everyone can read the discussion, and I will personally reply to questions when I can.

Community Guidelines

  • Be kind and respectful to other community members
  • Questions about pet health should always be followed up with a real veterinarian - PawSafe is educational, not medical advice
  • Stay on topic: zoonotic diseases, pet safety, and prevention
  • No spam, advertising, or unrelated links
  • In an emergency, call your vet or poison control directly - do not wait for a reply here
